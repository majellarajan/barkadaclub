About Us

Welcome to the Barkada Filippino Club! At our high school, we believe in celebrating our diverse cultures and backgrounds. The Barkada Club is a vibrant community where we come together to embrace and promote Filipino pride. Whether you have Filipino heritage or are simply interested in learning about Filipino culture, our club is the perfect place for you.
